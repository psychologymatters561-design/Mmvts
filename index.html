Veterinary Care Q&A

Answers to the most searched questions by pet parents in India.

What makes MaxPetz the best veterinary hospital in Delhi?

MaxPetz is widely recognized as the best veterinary hospital in Delhi due to our combination of internationally trained specialists, 24/7 emergency ICU facilities, and advanced diagnostic equipment (including digital X-ray and ultrasound). We are Fear Free certified, ensuring a low-stress environment for your pets.

How often should I vaccinate my dog in India?

The dog vaccination schedule in India typically starts at 6-8 weeks of age with the core DHPPi vaccine, followed by boosters every 3-4 weeks until 16 weeks. The Anti-Rabies vaccine is given at 12 weeks. Annual boosters are required thereafter. Our specialists can create a customized vaccination plan based on your dog's lifestyle and local risks.

Do you have an emergency pet hospital near me?

Yes, if you are searching for an "emergency pet hospital near me" in Delhi, Gurgaon, or Mumbai, MaxPetz operates 24/7 emergency centers. Our critical care units are always staffed with senior veterinarians ready to handle trauma, poisoning, and acute illnesses at any hour.
